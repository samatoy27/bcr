About Us

Quality Leather Repair

Blue Cliff Revival was founded with a passion for restoring leather goods to their former glory. We specialize in leather repair and upholstery, including vinyls, car seats, couches, and other furniture.

We take pride in using only the highest quality materials and techniques to ensure your leather items look as good as new. Our service can be provided in your home, business, or in our workspace.

Whether it's a family heirloom, your favorite chair, or your car's interior, we treat every project with the care and respect it deserves.
